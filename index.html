Commission Process
01
Share Your Idea

Tell us your concept — a character, a scene, a mood, a memory. The more detail you share, the closer the final art will be to your vision.

02
We Design It

You receive initial sketches and drafts. We refine together with your feedback until every line is exactly right.

03
Own It

Receive your final piece as a high-resolution digital file, physical print, or both — ready to display, gift, or sell.

About the Studio

Blacklines
Art Studios

A creative art studio based in Hyderabad, India — dedicated to the power of black and white. Every piece is crafted with obsessive attention to line, contrast, and detail. From bold character portraits to cinematic scene illustrations, the work lives where ink meets imagination.

Commissions are open. Whether you have a clear vision or just a feeling — bring it, and we'll turn it into something you'll keep forever.
